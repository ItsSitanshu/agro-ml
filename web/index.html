AgroML

Your one-stop solution for precision agriculture. Our cutting-edge machine learning model revolutionizes farming practices by offering three key functionalities. With AgroML, farmers can optimize crop health, enhance fertilizer efficiency, and make informed decisions to maximize their yields.

What we provide?

Disease Detection

At AgroML, we leverage advanced machine learning algorithms to detect and diagnose crop diseases with unparalleled accuracy. Our model analyzes plant images and identifies subtle signs of infections, enabling early intervention and targeted treatment.

Fertilizer Management

AgroML empowers farmers with precise fertilizer management solutions tailored to their specific soil conditions and crop requirements. By inputting soil data and other relevant parameters, our model recommends the optimal type of fertilizers and their respective proportions, ensuring efficient nutrient uptake and healthy crop growth.

Market Predictor

With AgroML's market prediction feature, farmers can make informed decisions based on real-time insights into crop prices and market trends. Our model analyzes historical data, weather patterns, and market dynamics to forecast future prices, enabling farmers to strategize their planting schedules and maximize profitability.
